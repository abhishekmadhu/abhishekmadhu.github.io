MARCH 05 2018

The Internship | SER - Ranchi

So, I was working at the Divisional Personnel Office, Ranchi Division, South Eastern Railway, as a Computer Science Intern for the last month.

The work primarily circled around developing a web application. We, the June-18 Batch had to apply offline, in hard copies for the internship. Our task was to digitise this process, with separate portals for the candidates and the admin, and a well optimised database

Work was fun, alongside a wonderful team to work with. On the plus side, the mentor assigned to us, Shri Ishwar Rao was really clear in his requirements, and strong in his guidance. We also recieved a lot of personal time from the kind Mr. Rishav Sinha (Asst. Personnel Officer, Ranchi) who helped us in adding functionality and features by pointing out the defeceits.

I also worked on a separate assignment , that initiated the digitisation of data of retired serviceholders. I created the framework, and also registered the data for a spsecific class of officers.
